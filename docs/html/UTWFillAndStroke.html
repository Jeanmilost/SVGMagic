ELineCap = (...);

Linecap enumeration

Values
  • E_LC_Default: The currently defined value is used
  • E_LC_Flat: The line ends at the last point, the end is squared off
  • E_LC_Square: Square cap, the center of the square is the last point in the line, the height and width of the square are the line width
  • E_LC_Round: Circular cap, the center of the circle is the last point in the line, the diameter of the circle is the line width
  • E_LC_Triangle: Triangular cap, the base of the triangle is the last point in the line, the base of the triangle is the line width
  • E_LC_NoAnchor: The line ends are not anchored
  • E_LC_SquareAnchor: The line ends are anchored with a square, the center of the square is the last point in the line, the height and width of the square are the line width
  • E_LC_RoundAnchor: The line ends are anchored with a circle, the center of the circle is at the last point in the line, the circle is wider than the line
  • E_LC_DiamondAnchor: The line ends are anchored with a diamond (a square turned at 45 degrees), the center of the diamond is at the last point in the line, the diamond is wider than the line
  • E_LC_ArrowAnchor: The line ends are anchored with arrowheads, the arrowhead point is located at the last point in the line, the arrowhead is wider than the line
  • E_LC_Custom: The line ends are made from a custom linecap
ELineJoin = (...);

Line join enumeration

Values
  • E_LJ_Default: The currently defined value is used
  • E_LJ_Round: Circular join. This produces a smooth, circular arc between the lines
  • E_LJ_Bevel: Beveled join. This produces a diagonal corner
  • E_LJ_Miter: Mitered join. This produces a sharp corner or a clipped corner, depending on whether the length of the miter exceeds the miter limit
  • E_LJ_MiterClipped: Mitered join. This produces a sharp corner or a beveled corner, depending on whether the length of the miter exceeds the miter limit
EWrapMode = (...);

Wrapping mode enumeration

Values
  • E_WM_Tile: The pattern is tiled
  • E_WM_TileFlipX: The pattern is flipped on the x axis and tiled
  • E_WM_TileFlipY: The pattern is flipped on the y axis and tiled
  • E_WM_TileFlipXY: The pattern is flipped on the both x and y axis and tiled
  • E_WM_Clamp: The pattern is clamped
